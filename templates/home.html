Clash Solver

Ingests your ACC clash report, proposes a resolution for every clash, and applies the ones you accept — straight into the live model.

Stops reporting clashes. Starts solving them.

Connect Clash Solver to your ACC project. It pulls the latest clash report, deduplicates against the previous run, and triages: real clash vs ghost (model-link drift, family swap, view-filter mismatch). For each real clash it proposes a resolution — re-route the smaller service, raise the ceiling void in the affected zone, swap a fitting, etc. — and shows you what the model would look like after.

You accept, refine, or reject each proposal. Accepted resolutions are applied to the live model as auditable Revit transactions (one-click undo). Rejected ones go into a 'why we kept this' log so the next sync doesn't propose the same thing.

CoBie Manager

Continuously populates and validates the COBie spreadsheet against the live Revit model. Every cell traceable to its source element.

A live COBie deliverable, never a panic before hand-over.

CoBie Manager is the COBie tool every BIM coordinator wishes Revit shipped with. Populates 20+ COBie sheets from the live model, validates against BS 1192-4 and your project EIR, and tracks every change with an audit log.

Every cell traces back to the source Revit element. No manual mapping, no copy-paste from schedules — the agent reads the model, fills the workbook, flags what's missing, and hands you a file ready for the client.

REPORT

Every recurring report — composed and traceable to source.

Report Builder turns the most repetitive part of an engineer's week into a five-minute review. Every report stays versioned, every claim is traceable to the source data — model, calcs, photos, minutes — and the formatting matches your house style.

Eight templates ship in the box: site report, MEP strategy, option appraisal, IESVE report, clash report, Stage 2 submission, Stage 3 submission, survey writeup. Point any template at a project and it pulls the model views, calc outputs, meeting minutes and site photos — and assembles the draft.

Specbuilder is the tool every MEP practice has tried to build in-house and given up on. It cross-references your live Revit model against the brief, applies your house standards, layers in your preferred manufacturer data, and writes a stage-appropriate specification.

Lock sections you've hand-edited, regenerate only what's changed downstream. NBS, CIBSE, ASHRAE or your own house template. Outputs to Word, PDF, or your DMS in one click.
