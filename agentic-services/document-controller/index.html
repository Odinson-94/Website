Why use AI Document Controller

The AI Document Controller watches your project folders and incoming email. When a transmittal arrives, the agent reads it, logs every document to the register with the correct revision, status and responsible party. No manual entry, no missed documents.

Outstanding approvals are chased on a configurable schedule. The agent knows who needs to sign off what, how long it's been waiting, and escalates when deadlines are approaching. Cover sheets are generated from templates — the agent fills in the metadata, you hit send.

The register dashboard gives you a live view of every document, its current status, revision history and any overdue items. Ask Jason in chat for a document status and the answer comes back with the full audit trail.

How it becomes agentic

We don't drop a tool on your desk and walk away. We move you up an automation maturity ladder over weeks, not years, with a human on every step until the agent has earned autonomy.

Stage 01

Connect

We connect the agent to your project folders, email and existing register. You define the naming conventions and approval workflows.

Stage 02

Shadow

The agent processes incoming transmittals alongside your existing DC for two weeks. You verify every register entry.

Stage 03

Assist

The agent handles registration and chasing autonomously. You review the dashboard and handle exceptions.

Stage 04

Autonomous

Full document control through the agent. Transmittals processed, approvals chased, cover sheets issued — you get alerts only when something needs a human decision.
