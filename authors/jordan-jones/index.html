About

Chartered Engineer (CEng BEng MCIBSE), founder and Chief Executive Officer of Adelphos AI. Author of the build-MEP automation engine that powers every Adelphos deliverable — apps, agentic services and bespoke automations across the building services design lifecycle.

Years of hands-on consultancy delivering complex construction projects across residential, retail, government and healthcare sectors led directly to the founding premise of Adelphos: software written by working professionals, with every workflow modelled on the actual logic applied on live projects.
