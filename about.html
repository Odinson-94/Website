About us. Software Built by Industry Professionals, for Industry Professionals.

Adelphos emerged from years of hands-on consultancy delivering complex construction projects across residential, retail, government and healthcare sectors. The founding team comprises practising professionals who have written the code themselves — every workflow reflects the actual logic applied on live construction projects. Optimisation through automation became the focus. By 2024, profit margins had grown from industry-typical 30% to over 70%. We codified our engineering logic, and the AI was born.
